Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Never share your OTP, passwords, or UPI PIN with anyone  
- Do not click on unknown or suspicious links  
- Always verify the sender before responding to messages or calls  
- Use strong and unique passwords for accounts  
- Enable two-factor authentication wherever possible  
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions  

UPI/Financial Safety Tips
- Never approve unknown payment requests on UPI apps  
- Always double-check the receiver’s details before sending money  

Report Cybercrime
- National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in  
- Helpline Number: 1930
