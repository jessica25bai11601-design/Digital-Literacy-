Social Media Do’s and Don’ts
Do’s
- Use respectful and professional language  
- Protect your personal information  
- Think before posting content  
- Use strong privacy settings  
- Share useful and positive content  
Don’ts
- Do not share passwords or OTPs  
- Avoid posting inappropriate or offensive content  
- Do not spread fake news or misinformation  
- Avoid engaging in online arguments  
- Do not click on suspicious or unknown links
